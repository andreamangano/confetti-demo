Share your stories.

Nowadays the easiest way to share something is creating a slide deck.

Doesn't matter the context where you are, slides play a very important role to capture the public attention and they allow to understand better what you are telling them.

Hundreds of new slide decks are published online everyday aiming to reach the biggest audience: the WEB! Slideshare, slides.com and Speaker Deck are only some examples of web sites that allow you to share presentations online.

Although they are great services that I recommend, some time ago I wondered if they are the best solution for the speakers' needs. My answer was no, they are not always.
